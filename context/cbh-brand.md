# CBH Homes — Brand Context File

---

## Brand Identity

- **Brand Name:** CBH Homes
- **Full Legal Name:** CBH Sales & Marketing, Inc.
- **Formerly:** Corey Barton Homes
- **Tagline:** "Built Different."
- **Sub-tagline options used in copy:** "Idaho's Number One Builder", "Built Different. Built for You."
- **Brand Personality:** Confident, approachable, community-rooted, value-driven, aspirational without being pretentious. Not corporate — Corey started this from a pickup truck, and that scrappiness and pride in craft is still core to the brand.
- **Tone of Voice:** Warm, direct, enthusiastic. Speaks like a knowledgeable friend, not a salesperson. Uses "you" and "your" heavily. Avoids jargon. Celebrates Idaho.

## Community Page Conventions (current CBH pattern)

Current community pages on cbhhomes.com include:
- Hero section with community name, price starting at, and quick CTAs
- Sticky top bar with promo and phone number
- In-page anchor nav: "Community Overview" | "Available Homes"
- Three promo tiles: Shop Homes / Self Tour / Buy Online
- Bullet list: "Why Live in [Community]?"
- Bullet list: "Why Choose CBH Homes?"
- Amenities list
- Directions
- Appointment scheduler embed
- Available homes grid
- Sales agent profiles with headshots
- FAQ accordion
- Lead capture form
- Pre-qual CTA

**Gaps in current pages (opportunities for the prototype to improve):**
- No quick stats bar (sq ft range, homes available, school district)
- No designer interiors / what's included section
- No narrative community story (lifestyle copy)
- No location proximity callouts
- No photo gallery section
- Conversion flow is flat — no hierarchy or visual urgency
- Mobile experience needs improvement
- FAQ is generic, not community-specific
- No schema markup visible

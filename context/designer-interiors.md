# Designer Interiors — Beaconwood at Eagle
## Level 5 & 6 Interior Selections

Beaconwood homes at Level 5 & 6 offer three designer colorways — **Cool**, **Neutral**, and **Warm** — each a fully coordinated palette of finishes selected by CBH's design team. Every selection is pre-curated, so buyers get a cohesive, professionally designed interior without the stress of choosing individual finishes.

> "All selections are subject to change without notice. Buyer to confirm selections at cabinet install date with a CBH Sales Specialist."

---

## Colorway 1: COOL

**Vibe:** Moody, modern, sophisticated. Dark wood tones meet crisp whites and cool grays with matte black hardware throughout.

| Category | Selection |
|---|---|
| Greyloch Cabinet | Riviera Oak Cassis |
| Interior Paint | First Star RP7646 |
| Quartz Countertops | Miami White |
| Kitchen Backsplash | Passion Blanco |
| Bath Backsplash | Telento Gris |
| Flooring | Monarch, Beaver Creek |
| Carpet | Elite |
| Tub / Fireplace Tile | 12x24 Llistone Gray |
| Cabinet Hardware | Matte Black Bar Pull |
| Kitchen Faucet | Matte Black Pull-Down |
| Bathroom Faucet | Matte Black Single-Handle |

**Design Notes:** The Riviera Oak Cassis cabinet is a dark, weathered wood-look finish. Paired with Miami White quartz (bright white, minimal veining) and matte black hardware, this palette creates a high-contrast, editorial kitchen feel. The Telento Gris bath backsplash adds cool blue-gray marble movement. Llistone Gray tile grounds the tub and fireplace surround.

## Shared Across All Colorways

- **Cabinet brand:** Greyloch (all three colorways use Greyloch cabinetry, just in different door styles/finishes)
- **Paint brand:** Interior paint is Benjamin Moore (RP codes = BM Regal Select)
  - Cool + Neutral: First Star RP7646 (soft warm white)
  - Warm: Crushed Ice RP7647 (slightly cooler crisp white)
- **Quartz:** All countertops are quartz (brand TBD — confirm with CBH)
- **Flooring type:** All LVP (luxury vinyl plank) — confirm brand with CBH
- **Carpet:** All homes include carpet in bedrooms
- **Tile format:** All tub/fireplace tile is 12x24 format
